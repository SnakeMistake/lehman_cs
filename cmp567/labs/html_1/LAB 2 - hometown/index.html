Birmingham, AL

Location

Birmingham is located in central Alabama in the heart of the American South. It's about 2 hours North of Montgomery and about 1.5 hours south of Huntsville. Atlanta is also a few hours to the East in Georgia.

About

Culture

Birmingham's culture is certainly southern, but the commercial hub of downtown Birmingham also draws arts and culture from across the world.

Food

Birmingham is famous for Barbeque and Soul Food. The fried green tomatoes and boiled peanuts are a highlight.

Music

The south may be primarily known for folk and country music, but Birmingham draws major pop music and indie rock acts as well.

Things to Do

  • Hike at Oak Mountain
  • Watch College Football
  • Visit the Civil Rights Museum
